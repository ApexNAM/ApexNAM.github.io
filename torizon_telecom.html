Torizon : Telecom

Torizon: Telecom is a first person shooting game (FPS)

in the form of a speedrun that escapes the world surrounded by text.

Story

In this world, "Telecom" is a whole world made up of text. Even the sky is made up of

eternal black and dark colors. The protagonist feels demonstrated to this world and must

move on, resisting programs that try to control you, in order to move to a better world.

The telecom world is made up of text.

Things... buildings... any living thing... even you... surrounded by text.

If it's really terrible, we should move to an innovative world.
